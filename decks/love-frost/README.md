# Love Frost (爱霜)

A beautiful Chinese song about eternal love and waiting through time.

## Song Structure

The song tells a story of enduring love across lifetimes, with themes of:
- **Nature imagery**: Wind, moonlight, sunset
- **Time concepts**: Thousands of years, lifetimes
- **Romantic elements**: Love letters, meetings, promises
- **Poetic expressions**: Red lotus, fragrant time

## Learning Progression

- **Level 1**: Basic vocabulary (1-2 characters) - Essential words and components
- **Level 2**: Short phrases (3-5 characters) - Key expressions and combinations  
- **Level 3**: Complete lines - Full lyric lines with bridge phrases for longer sections

## Key Vocabulary Themes

1. **Time and eternity**: 万年, 千年, 时光
2. **Love and relationships**: 相爱, 相遇, 情书
3. **Nature elements**: 风, 月光, 斜阳, 红莲
4. **Actions and emotions**: 等待, 歌唱, 弹奏, 染香
