Verse 1:
在你輝煌得時刻讓我為你唱首歌
zai ni huihuang de shike
At the moment of your memories

讓我為你唱首歌
rang wo wei ni chang shou ge
Let me sing a song for you

我的好兄弟心裡有苦你對我說
wo de hao xiongdi
My good brother

心裡有苦你對我說
xin li you ku ni dui wo shuo
You told me your heartache

前方大路一起走 哪怕是河也一起過
qianfang dalu yiqi zou
Let's walk together towards the mainland

那怕是河也一起過
napa shi he ye yiqiguo
Even if it's a river, we'll cross it together

苦點累點又能算什麼
ku dian lei dian you neng suan shenme
A little bitterness, a few tears, what does it matter

Verse 2:
在你需要我的時候 我來陪你一起度過
zai ni xuyao wo de shihou
When you need me

我來陪你一起度過
wo lai pei ni yiqi duguo
I'll be there to accompany you

我的好兄弟心裡有苦你對我說
wo de hao xiongdi
My good brother

心裡有苦你對我說
xin li you ku ni dui wo shuo
You told me your heartache

人生難得起起落落 還是要堅強的生活
rensheng nande qi qi luoluo
Life is rarely smooth

還是要堅強的生活
haishi yao jianqiang de shenghuo
We still have to live strong

哭過笑過至少你還有我
kuguo xiaoguo zhishao ni hai you wo
Suffering is less effective when you have me

Chorus:
朋友的情誼呀比天還高比地還遼闊
pengyou de qingyi ya bi tian hai gao bi de hai liaokuo
The friendship surpasses the sky and the sea

那些歲月我們一定會記得
naxie suiyue women yiding hui jide
Those moments we will always remember

朋友的情誼呀我們今生最大的難得
pengyou de qingyi ya women jinsheng zuida de nande
Friendship surpasses the greatest difficulties in life

像一杯酒 像一首老歌
xiang yibei jiu xiang yi shou lao ge
Like a glass of wine, like an old song
